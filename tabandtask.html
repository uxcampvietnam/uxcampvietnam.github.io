I. Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 9, 2025

Tab & Task is a Chrome extension designed to help you manage your tabs, and to-do lists more efficiently. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Access

Tab & Task operates entirely within your browser. It uses the following Chrome APIs to provide its features:

  • tabs – to list and manage your open tabs.
  • storage – to locally save your settings, notes, or to-do items.

All data related to your tabs, and to-do items is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or sell any personal information to third parties.

Optional Analytics (Mixpanel)

To improve user experience, Tab & Task optionally uses Mixpanel for anonymous usage analytics. Analytics are disabled by default and can be enabled through the extension’s settings panel (opt-in).

When analytics are enabled, the extension may collect:

  • Anonymous events such as “created to-do”, “saved link”, “opened tab”
  • General usage frequency (e.g., number of actions performed)

We do not collect or transmit personally identifiable information, website content, or sensitive data. Specifically, the text you type in your to-do list and the actual URLs of your tabs are never sent to Mixpanel or any other service.

Data Storage and Retention

All user data, settings, and saved lists are stored locally in Chrome’s extension storage system. When you uninstall the extension, all stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

Your Control

You can view, clear, or disable analytics data collection at any time within the extension settings. You may also remove all data by uninstalling the extension or clearing Chrome’s extension storage.

Third-Party Services

Apart from Mixpanel (used only when enabled by you), Tab & Task does not integrate with any external APIs, analytics, or advertising platforms.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect product or legal changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
