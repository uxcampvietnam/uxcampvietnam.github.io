Part I: Terms and Conditions

1. Introduction

The Tab & Task Supabase Sync Extension is a productivity tool designed to help you organize browser tabs and manage tasks. It features local storage and cloud synchronization through Supabase integration, allowing for cross-device access and collaboration.

2. Permissions

This Extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your preferences, settings, and local tab/task data on your device.
  • Tabs: To list, organize, and manage your open browser tabs.
  • Host Permissions (https://*.supabase.co/*): To connect securely with Supabase for data synchronization.

We only use these permissions for their intended purposes and never access or distribute your private browsing data.

3. User Responsibilities

By using this Extension, you agree to:

  • Use the Extension in compliance with all applicable laws.
  • Not use the Extension for illegal, harmful, or malicious purposes.
  • Not attempt to reverse engineer or interfere with the Extension's functionality.
  • Be responsible for the content you save or share within the Extension.

4. Collaboration and Shared Folders

The Extension allows you to share folders with other users. Please be aware of the following roles:

  • Owners: Can create, read, update, and delete all content in a shared folder, including managing members.
  • Editors: Can add, modify, or delete tabs and tasks within shared folders. Actions taken by editors are visible to the owner and other collaborators.
  • Viewers: Have read-only access to the shared folders.

5. Public Access

Users have the option to make specific folders "Public."

  • Public folders are accessible to anyone who has the unique public link.
  • No authentication is required to view the content of a public folder.
  • Ensure that you do not include sensitive information in folders you choose to make public.

Part II: Privacy Policy

We take a privacy-first approach:

  • No PII: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, or browsing history.
